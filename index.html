Research Area 지능형차량 신호처리 실험실(IVSP Lab)은 자율주행을 구성하는 다양한 요소기술들에 관한 연구를 진행하고 있습니다.

Perception

Perception uses sensors to continuously scan and monitor the environment, similar to human vision and other senses.

  • Camera Deep Learning (YOLO)
  • Lidar Deep Learning (VoxelNet)
  • Object Detection
  • Semantic Segmentation
  • Camera/LiDAR/Radar/Ultrasonic Sensor Fusion (tightly,  loosely-coupled)

Lacalization and mapping

Localization and mapping algorithms calculate the global and local location of the ego-vehicle and map the environment from sensor data and other perception outputs.

  • SLAM(Simultaneous Localization and Mapping)
  • Visual SLAM
  • LiDAR SLAM
  • VINS
  • Structure from Motion (SFM)
  • Multi-View Geometry (MVG)

Path planning

Path planning determines possible safe routes for the ego-vehicle based on perception, and localization and mapping information.

  • A*
  • RRT
  • Elastic Band
  • MPC
  • Potential Field
